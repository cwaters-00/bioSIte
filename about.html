About Carter

Carter is a security professional in the corporate sector currently by occupation, but cultivates and expresses himself through his various crafts. Born on December 18th, 1995, in chicago illinois carter grew up in a home that not only allowed his creativity to flourish but also encouraged and supported it. His parents' strong mechanical inclination led him to take a very physical, technical approach to expressing himself in the world.

It began by dismantling his toys as a youth, but grew into making things out of spare material he could acquire or restoring stray guitars gifted to him

he attended several extended summer courses in Carbondale, Illinois, to work with architecture students on campus, as well as being a 4-year member of the ACE mentor program, a program designed to get young adults involved in the trades by placing them with professionals in their fields for projects during and outside of the school year.

In 2014, he enrolled in a private engineering college in Milwaukee, but returned home due to financial strain, only to complete his degree, showcasing his commitment and resilience in his craft.
