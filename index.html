About Carter Carter's Hobbies

Carter

Carter is at his core a maker. He is someone who sees the potential in objects and materials and seeks to bring them out. Starting as a youth, he attended several architecture courses/camps in southern Illinois, a high school in Chicago that was uniquely devoted to teaching its students about the trades in a classroom setting and even using the school's workshop to design, develop, and sell products ranging from furniture to guitar picks. He now works in corporate security but often indulges in his various crafts.
